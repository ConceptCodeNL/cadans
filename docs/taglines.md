# CADANS Tagline Suggestions

## Top 5 Taglines (with Check-in Focus)

### 1. **"Check-in. Grade. Grow."**
- **Why it works**: Three-action structure, emphasizes the check-in process as the foundation, clear progression
- **Tone**: Direct, professional, results-focused
- **Use case**: Main tagline, homepage hero, marketing materials
- **Length**: Short, memorable, action-oriented

### 2. **"Regular Check-ins. Better Grades."**
- **Why it works**: Emphasizes the value of consistent check-ins leading to improved outcomes
- **Tone**: Professional, benefit-focused, clear value proposition
- **Use case**: Marketing materials, feature highlights
- **Length**: Clear and benefit-driven

### 3. **"Your Check-in Cadence"**
- **Why it works**: Combines "check-in" with "cadence" (rhythm), emphasizes structured, consistent approach
- **Tone**: Personal, professional, rhythmic
- **Use case**: Alternative tagline, emphasizes consistency
- **Length**: Memorable and brand-aligned

### 4. **"Check-in with Confidence"**
- **Why it works**: Emphasizes ease and reliability of the check-in process, reassuring tone
- **Tone**: Approachable, confident, supportive
- **Use case**: User-focused messaging, onboarding
- **Length**: Simple and reassuring

### 5. **"Check-ins That Matter"**
- **Why it works**: Emphasizes meaningful, valuable check-ins rather than just routine tasks
- **Tone**: Purposeful, meaningful, value-driven
- **Use case**: Marketing, emphasizes quality over quantity
- **Length**: Memorable and impactful

## Tagline Usage Guidelines

### Logo Integration
- **Short taglines** (3-4 words): Best for logo integration
- **Medium taglines** (5-7 words): Good for marketing materials
- **Long taglines** (8+ words): Use in descriptions, not logos

### Current Logo Tagline
Currently using: **"GRADING PLATFORM"**
- Simple and descriptive
- Can be replaced with any of the top 5 suggestions
- Works well in both English and Dutch

### Multilingual Considerations
- Taglines should translate well to Dutch
- Consider cultural nuances
- May need separate taglines for EN/NL if direct translation doesn't work

## Recommended Implementation

### Primary Tagline
**"Check-in. Grade. Grow."** or **"Your Check-in Cadence"**

### Secondary Taglines (for different contexts)
- Marketing: "Regular Check-ins. Better Grades."
- User-focused: "Check-in with Confidence"
- Value-driven: "Check-ins That Matter"
- Alternative: "Grade with Rhythm" (if check-in focus not needed)

### Logo Options
1. **With tagline**: Full logo with tagline below "CADANS"
2. **Without tagline**: Just "CADANS" text (cleaner, more minimal)
3. **Contextual**: Use different taglines in different sections

## Testing Recommendations

1. **A/B Testing**: Test different taglines with target users
2. **Feedback**: Gather feedback from teachers, administrators
3. **Clarity**: Ensure tagline clearly communicates value
4. **Memorability**: Test recall and recognition
5. **Brand Alignment**: Ensure tagline matches brand personality
